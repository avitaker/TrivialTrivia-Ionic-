This is a multiple-choice trivia quiz with 10 questions

Each question has a twenty-second time limit

You can view your progress and the current question's category in the bottom bar

Tap your chosen answer to submit it. The first selected answer is submitted

When the timer runs out, the next question will be loaded and no points awarded

You can view a number of statistics about your performance in the "Statistics" page

That's it! I hope you enjoy the game
